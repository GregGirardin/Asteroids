An asteroids type game.

Objective

  Shoot as many aliens and asteroids in the three waves.

Scoring

  Tanker making it safely away 300
  Small Attacking aliens       200
  Big aliens                   100
  Asteroids                     10
  Cannons cost 1 and Torpedos cost 10

Keys
  up = accelerate
  down = slow down
  left = turn left
  right = turn right
  space = fire cannon
  t = fire torpedo

A refueling tanker will appear occasionally to replenish resources.
